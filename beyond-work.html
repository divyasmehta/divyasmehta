Off Screen

Not a Netflix person

You won't find me binge-watching — I haven't watched a movie in seven years. My idea of a good evening is hosting friends at home: cooking for them, trying a new vegan recipe, and losing track of time over board games. I'm drawn to theatre — not just the play, but everything that happens behind it — and I've done a few acting workshops to get closer to that world. Beyond that, it's poetry open mics and long drives.

Yoga

Practicing balance, on and off the mat

Yoga started as a practice and became a lens. Bringing its aspects into daily life — breath, discipline, stillness — is quietly making me a better person. I take solo trips to yoga schools around India: a month in Rishikesh for my 200-hour teacher training, and another 100 hours across Mysore and Goa.
